Background

We had a bunch of Google Sheets and online entities over the years.

The information was compiled and split into seven entity types:

  • Announcements
  • Hacknights
  • Organizations
  • People
  • Projects
  • Resources
  • Venues

Further definition was implemented with categorization via tagging.
